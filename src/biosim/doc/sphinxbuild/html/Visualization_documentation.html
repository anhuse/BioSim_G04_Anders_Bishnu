The visualization Class and its Subclasses¶

class visualization.Visualization[source]¶

Plotting island map, heatmaps of herbivore and carnivore distribution and line graph of herbivore and carnivore count.

Variables:
  • _fig – plt.figure(default, None), figure to put subplots in
  • _map_ax – (default, None) plot of the island map
  • _img_axis – (default, None) axis to _map_ax
  • _herb_ax – heatmap(default, None), plot for herbivore distribution
  • _carn_ax – heatmap(default, None), plot for carnivore distribution
  • _mean_ax – (default, None) plot for the linegraphs
  • _herb_line – (default, None) herbivore-count line in _mean_ax
  • _carn_line – (default, None) carnivore-count line in _mean_ax
  • _herb_axis – (default, None) axis to _herb_ax
  • _carn_axis – (default, None) axis to _carn_ax
